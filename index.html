Welcome to RMSTUSC

Rangamati Science & Technology University Science Club (RMSTUSC; acronym) is a prominent science-based social organization operated by the students of Rangamati Science & Technology University.


It was established with a vision to spread the blessings of science across the country. Rangamati Science & Technology University Science Club is committed to achieving its visions and aims with the highest efficiency. It not only tests academic excellence but also creates a space for critical dialogue, innovation, and networking among budding scientists.


A written constitution is an integral part of an organization for functioning properly. This constitution is formulated to make a proper structure of the club administration and workflow. This constitution is a result of lots of hard work of the contributors. Each member of Rangamati Science & Technology University Science Club believes in change..


At RMSTUSC, we believe in nurturing leadership through science. From mentoring school students to collaborating with national and international science communities, our impact goes beyond campus boundaries. Join us as we continue to explore, discover, and inspire the next generation of scientific minds.


Mission:

The purpose of this club shall be to work on skill development, acquire knowledge, and ignite scientific curiosity and innovation, especially among the people of the Hill Tracts, and actively taking part in a scientific and technological revolution for the welfare of nature and human.


Vision:

We operate under a multidisciplinary structure,Research and Development, Human Resource and Public Affairs, Promotion and Publication,Project and Events IT and Social Activities

Rangamati Science and Technology University proudly hosts a Science Club that is officially registered under the Rangamati Science and Technology University(RMSTU). The club is dedicated to nurturing curiosity, creativity, and innovation among students by engaging them in various scientific, technological, and research-based activities. Through workshops, seminars, and projects, it creates opportunities for young minds to explore science beyond the classroom and contribute to building a knowledge-based society.
